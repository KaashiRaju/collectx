CollectX - Collections & Recovery Management 
System
1. Introduction
CollectX is a web-based Collections & Recovery Management System designed for banks, 
NBFCs, and fintech lenders to manage end-to-end delinquency handling—from strategy 
segmentation, queue allocation, dunning orchestration, promise-to-pay (PTP) tracking, 
field collections, payment posting references, settlements, repossessions, to write-off 
and post write-off recoveries.
It supports retail and SME loan products (secured/unsecured) and integrates with core loan 
servicing (Phase-1 as internal references only). The platform provides risk-based strategies, 
contact policies, agent performance tracking, and regulatory grade audit trails.
It uses a REST API-based backend and Angular/React frontend, compatible with Java 
(Spring Boot) or .NET (ASP.NET Core).
Actors / Users
• Collections Agent (Inbound/Outbound): Works assigned queues, captures 
contacts, PTPs, and payments.
• Field Collections Officer: Executes field visits, repossessions, and document 
serving.
• Collections Supervisor: Manages strategy assignment, queue balancing, and 
approvals.
• Recovery Manager: Manages settlements, legal/agency placements, post write-off 
recoveries.
• Compliance & QA Analyst: Reviews call logs, contact policy adherence, audit trails.
• Admin: Configures products, strategies, scorecards, users/roles, and reports.
2. Module Overview
2.1 Identity & Access Management
Authentication, RBAC, and audit logs.
2.2 Portfolio Ingestion & Delinquency Staging
Ingest loan accounts, compute DPD, bucketization, risk score references.
2.3 Strategy, Segmentation & Queue Allocation
Strategy rules, segmentation, agent capacity and territory allocation.
2.4 Dunning Orchestration & Contact Policies
Channel cadence (in-app placeholders), contact attempts, consent and preferences.
2.5 Agent Workbench & Case Handling
360-view, call logs, PTPs, disputes, hardship flags, follow-ups.
2.6 Payments, PTP & Settlements
Promise management, payment references, settlements/waivers approvals.
2.7 Field Collections, Repossession & Agency Management
Visit scheduling, GPS notes (ref), repossession workflow, agency placements.
2.8 Legal, Write-Off & Post Write-Off Recoveries
Notices, legal case references, charge-off, recovery accounting references.
2.9 Reporting, KPIs & Performance Management
Roll rates, cure rates, PTP kept, cash collected, agent productivity.
2.10 Notifications & Alerts
PTP due, bucket roll risk, approvals, compliance flags.
3. Architecture Overview
• Frontend: Angular or React
• Backend: REST API microservices
• Database: Relational (MySQL/PostgreSQL/SQL Server)
• Integrations (Phase-1): Core loan & payment systems simulated as references; 
dialer/SMS/email not integrated (in-app only)
